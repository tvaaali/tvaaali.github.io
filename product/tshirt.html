About This Product

Get your favorite yellow polo t-shirt customized with embroidered message!

Specifications:
  • Style No : Polo
  • Sleeves : Half Sleeves
  • Description : Embroidered Polo
  • Max. Embroidered Size : 10cm x 10cm
  • Color : Yellow
  • Available sizes: S-M-L-XL-XXL
  • Hand or Machine wash separately in cold water. Use no bleach. Do not dry clean, gentle cycle. Do not Tumble Dry. Remove promptly touch up with a cool iron if needed.

Embroidery Recommendations:
  • Avoid small/tiny letters and designs.
  • The designs/logo uploaded should have a resolution of 300 dpi.
  • Embroidery is recommended for designs in solid colors only.
  • As the embroidered design is sewn with thread, we recommend you to upload the large and clear graphics.
  • Don't use gradient in graphics or text.

  • Note: Embroidery provides best results for LOGO and Text only, so please ensure uploaded artwork includes only logo or text.


When personalizing your model, please pay attention to the following aspects:

  • Review your input: it cannot be change after approval!
  • If you are uploading artwork or logos, please pay attention to the resolution of the images: their resolution must be of 300dpi.
  • Low resolution files will not result in great printing quality.
  • There might be a slight color change between your computer screen and the paper printing.
